About Kigali

Kigali is the capital and largest city of Rwanda, known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and stunning landscapes. The city offers a unique blend of traditional and modern experiences, making it a must-visit destination for travelers.

Top things to do in kigali

  • visit the Kigali Genocide Memorial
  • visit Inema Arts Center
  • shop at kimironko market
  • Enjoy local cuisine
  • visit the Kigali City Tour

Kigali Guide: Insider Tips for Travelers

  1. Getting Around: Use safe and affordable moto-taxis or ride-hailing apps to explore the city.
  2. Best Views: Head up Mount Kigali at sunset for breathtaking panoramas of the city’s rolling hills.
  3. Local Flavor: Try Rwandan specialties like brochettes and ibirayi at neighborhood restaurants.
  4. Hidden Gems: Visit Inema Arts Center for contemporary art and live performances.
  5. Community Vibe: Join a weekend car-free day to walk, cycle, and mingle with locals.
  6. Respect & Culture: Kigali is known for its cleanliness and order — follow local customs and enjoy the welcoming atmosphere.
